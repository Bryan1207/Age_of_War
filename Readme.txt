Instructies;

The goal of the game is to survive and destroy the enemy base.

The game is devided in 5 ages. To move to the next age, you need 
XP points. To gain these points, you have to kill enemy units.
You also gain XP points when one of your units is killed. You can
also build defences. Finding the balance between defence and 
offence is the key.

You will also be able to use a special attack. This attack will need
tim to be available again after you use it. Each age have it's own 
special attack.

You cannot repair you base, but it will gain health points 
everytime you evolve to the next age. Protect your base at all costs!
